“Yaad h kese hm sab cake se holi khelne me lage the pr ek tha jo ekdum hi mana kr rha tha cake lagwane se. Haa samanth hi tha wo. Fir yaad h tujhe use kese cake lagaya tha mene? Use dekh kr tu hasi bhot thi... teri wo hasi dhyaan aati h to mann hi mann bhot achha lgta h mujhe.”

Or bhi kuch cheeze hui thi. Yaad kr us din mujhe kisine bday wish kiya tha!
