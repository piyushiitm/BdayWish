Happiest birthday to my dear Chhutki...

Haa pta h mujhe, mere kuch time ke behaviour se tujhe ye baat jhooth lag rhi hogi. But shi me, a very very very very happy birthday to you.

Tu janti to h hi mujhe. Jitna meri life me kya chal rha h tu janti h, utna to shayad hi koi or janta ho. You know every aspect of my situation. Me krna nhi chahta, wesa behave apne aap krne lag gya hu, or me dil se maafi chahta hu. Chahta hu tu mujhe maaf kre, me dil se chahta hu ki me tujhko maaf krdu, or in sab cheezo se aage badh kr khud bhi khush rahu or tujhe khush krne ke liye bhi sab kuch kar paau jesa me aaj tk krta aaya hu. And I promise you I will try my best.

Yaad h pichli baar wala letter? Kuch waade kiye the mene unme, pr bhagwan se kuch maanga bhi tha tere liye...

"May God remove every obstacle that obstructs you, be it me."

And I believe shayad isiliye ham dono ke beech ki dooriyan badh gyi h. But I promise you one more thing today: I will try my best to not be that obstacle and be a staircase for you to climb upon to the wishes of your life.

Bhot kuch kiya tune mere liye bhi, kuch cheeze kar nhi paayi. Par mene un kuch cheezo ke liye tujhe zaroorat se bhot jada suna diya or bhot baar. Me iske liye tujhse maafi maangna chahta hu or abhi mangta bhi hu. And I promise you from today onwards I will never speak in such manner. If I am ever angry on you, I will take a break, gather my calm, and then will come talk to you.

Please let go of everything bad that has happened between us, and with me contribute to those huge collection of memories that we uphold together as one of the best memories of our life.

Tune mujhko mana kar diya gift dene ke liye, kaha to enjoy nhi kr paati. To isiliye mene is baari ye cheez tujhe dene ke liye socha. Ye website hamari bhot sari memories ko mene sanjo kr banayi h. I really hope tujhko pasand aaygi or tera ye birthday bhi yaadgaar hoga.

Or me chahta hu tera ye saal pichle saal se bilkul hatke rahe, sab bhot achha ho, or ham log khub sari memories banaye sath me. Once again, a Very Very Very Happy Birthday To My Dear Chhutki.
